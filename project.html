About Us

Every year, millions of forgetful partners have to face their significant others' disappointment when they come home empty-handed. We want to provide a gift service where anyone can borrow one of our 20 Cupid Delivery Couriers to get them a gift before heading home. No need to leave work or panic when we're on the case!
